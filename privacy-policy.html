The Application collects information when you download and use it. This information may include:

The Application uses AdMob, a mobile advertising service provided by Google. AdMob may use cookies and similar technologies to collect information about your device and usage patterns to provide personalized advertisements.

You can opt-out of personalized advertising by visiting your device's advertising settings.

Location Information

The Application uses approximate location data to provide atmospheric conditions (temperature, pressure, altitude) for ballistic calculations. This data is:

  • Used only for calculation purposes
  • Not stored on our servers
  • Not shared with third parties except as required for the Application's functionality
  • Optional - you can manually enter atmospheric data instead
